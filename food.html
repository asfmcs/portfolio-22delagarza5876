Recipe #1: S'mores

  • Time: 2 min
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Graham crackers 2 halves
Hershey Milk chocolate 1 half
Marshmallows 2

Step 1: Put one half of the graham cracker on a plate that can be put on the microwave

Step 2: Put the half of the Hershey chocolate bar o top of the graham cracker on the plate

Step 3: Put the two marshmallows on top of the chocolate and the graham cracker on the plate (evenly inside of the chocolate)

Step 4: Microwave for 30 seconds

Step 5: After being in the microwave, take out and put the other half of the graham cracker on top of the product.

Step 6: Enjoy




Recipe #2: Limeaid

  • Time: 3-5 min
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Water 1 cup
Lime 2
Sugar 1 tsp
Spoon 1
Sharp knife 1
Lime squeezer 1
Cutting board 1

Step 1: Grab the cutting board and all of the limes. Cut them in half using the sharp knife.

Step 2: With the lime squeezer, squeeze all of the halves into the cup to have lime juice.

Step 3: Add 1 tsp of sugar into the cup, were the lime juice is as well.

Step 4: Add 1 cup of water into the mix (the cup)

Step 5: With the spoon, mix for about 2 minutes until the sugar is completly disolved.

Step 6: Enjoy




Recipe #3: Papas Preparadas

  • Time: 3-5 min
  • Serves: 3-4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Plain chips (whatever brand) 1 bag
Lime 1
Lime squeezer 1
Sharp knife 1
Big bowl 1
Cutting board 1
Botanera 1
Chile La Fina or Tajin 1
Salsa Maggie 1
Salsa Inglesa 1

Step 1: Grab the cutting board and the lime. Cut the lime in half using the sharp knife.

Step 2: Grab the bowl and put the whole bag of chips inside of the plate.

Step 3: Add in the botanera sauce on top of all of the chips (try to make it reach all of the chips) and add by your own taste.

Step 4: Mix by shaking the bowl up and down and side by side (gently) and add more botanera if needed.

Step 5: Add salsa Inglesa to all of the chips (do not put as much this time, but enough for it to reach all of the chips) and add by your own taste.

Step 6: Mix by shaking the bowl up and down and side by side (gently) and add more salsa Inglesa if needed.

Step 7: Add salsa Maggie just like the salsa Inglesa (not as much but enough for all of the chips). Add by your own taste.

Step 8: Mix by shaking the bowl up and down and side by side (gently) and add more salsa Maggie if needed.

Step 8: With the lime squeezer, add in both halves of the lime and mix again.

Step 9: Enjoy.
